Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers before you submit

How fast will my ad be ready?

Digital ads are typically prepared within 48 hours or by the second business day after this agreement is signed. Business days are Monday through Friday.

What if I do not have an ad ready yet?

Select the free ad creation option and Ownly Marketing will create the ad for you at no additional charge using the business details and files you submit.

What is included in print plus digital?

Print plus digital includes postcard distribution to your selected market, featured digital placement tied to the campaign, final approval due at least 7 days before the scheduled mailing drop, and mailing submitted on the first scheduled mailing date of the month after approval requirements are met.

When do refunds apply?

Print services are final after 72 hours because of custom design, print preparation, and mailing production. Digital-only service, available for national advertisers only, may be refunded within 72 hours of purchase.

Who qualifies for digital-only service?

Digital-only service is available for national advertisers only. Local-market digital-only placements are not offered in order to avoid competing business conflicts.

How do I cancel digital-only recurring service?

Send your cancellation request to admin@ownly1nce.com or call 941-277-9876 at least 72 hours before your next billing date.

Are marketing results guaranteed?

No. Response, leads, and return on investment can vary based on the market, offer, timing, creative, and advertiser responsiveness.
